About Me

I Like to Get My Content On!

I created my first video on an RCA VHS camcorder in 1991; it was a somewhat incomprehensible story involving my Ninja Turtles action figures, who were portraying characters from Star Trek. There were many more, and with the help of friends I got an early appreciation for the beauty of overdubbing (especially when you don’t have access to a non-linear editor), the immense patience required for stop-motion animation, and how good sound is as important as good visuals.

I created my first digital music recording in 1996 by overlaying a MIDI sequence with additional production on my Yamaha keyboard. It was the first of many audio projects, ranging from solo recordings to multitrack productions. I eventually built a respectable project studio in my 30’s. I continue to play music and record it when I feel like my chops are up.

I built my first web page in 1999 and ventured into digital photography in the early 2000’s. I’ve built and maintained several web pages and sites since. My continuing education includes training in full-stack web development. I view it as an expansion of my interest in digital media.
